opWin - Investigate Market Information, and Evaluate Your Strategy

showcase of a TAIEX Quant application

View on GitHub Download .zip Download .tar.gz

Easy Market Information Update

  • One command updates all

View and Compare Information In Any Period

  • Zoom-in/Zoom-out to browse market information in any period from 1993
  • Investigate two TAIEX information simultaneously

View and Compare TAIEX and Stock Information Simultaneously

  • Compare selected stock information with TAIEX
  • Any pair of daily/weekly/monthly/quarterly information can be viewed in unified time period

Efficient Strategy Backtesting

  • Large amount of information (>100M) can be handled in seconds in a virtual environment
  • Backtesting result is presented in terms of net asset value, yearly ROR, sharpe ratio, monthly ROR distribution, ..., and many others.

View and Compare Backtesting Results with Market Information

Technology

python:

  • Unified data retrieval for various raw format (csv, htm, xls)
  • Three-level cache hierarchy: from ungrouped raw format to grouped binary pickle format
  • Unittest framework by mocking

qt/qwt:

  • Portable pure GUI application
  • Real-time data visualization
  • Seamless strategy specification, evaluation, and result comparison

Boost: Python binding, serialization

Google Test: Smoke testing framework
